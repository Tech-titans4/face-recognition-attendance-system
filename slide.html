ABOUT

*

The proposed project is an automated attendance marking and management system which makes use of face detection and recognition algorithms.

Faces are recognized using face recognition algorithms.

*

Face recognition attendance system automatically identifies and confirms a person and records attendance based on their face detection.

The main objective of this work is to make the attendance marking and management system efficient, time saving, simple and easy.

*

The processed image will then be compared against the existing stored record and then attendance is marked in the database accordingly.

HOW
IT
WORKS?

*

Step-1: Switch on the webcam and wait till the face gets recognized

Step-2: Once the face gets recognized the photo, attendance, ID,Major,Grade,Year of study and year of joining of the student will be displayed

*

Step-3: A marked signal gets displayed

Step-4: IF the same person tries to give the attendance again it displays as already marked.

*

Once the attendance is marked the attendance counter and the last update date and time gets updated in the database.
